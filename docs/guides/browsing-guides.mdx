## Browse All Guides

Visit the [Guides page](/guides) to see all published guides. Guides are displayed in reverse chronological order with the newest guides first.

Each guide card shows:
- **Title** and description
- **Author** information
- **Reactions** count
- **Published date**

## Search for Guides

### Quick Search

Use the search bar in the navigation for instant results:

1. Click the search icon or press `/` to focus the search bar
2. Type your query (minimum 3 characters)
3. See instant results as you type
4. Click a result to view the full guide

<Tip>
  **Pro tip**: Quick search is perfect for finding guides when you know exactly what you're looking for.
</Tip>

## Save Guides

### Bookmarking

Save guides for quick access later:

1. Open any guide
2. Click the **bookmark icon** in the top right
3. The guide is instantly saved to your account

### Access Saved Guides

View all your bookmarked guides:
- From your [Dashboard](/dashboard)
- From the [Saved Guides](/saved-guides) page
- Click the bookmark icon again to unsave

<Info>
  **Saved guides** are private to you. Other users cannot see what you've saved.
</Info>
