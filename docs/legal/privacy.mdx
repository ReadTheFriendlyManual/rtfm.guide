RTFM.guide ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

- Profile information (bio, location, social links - when available)

**Content You Create**
- Guides you write
- Comments you post
- Reactions you give
- Guides you save

**Communications**
- Support requests
- Feedback and surveys
- Reports and appeals

### Automatically Collected Information

**Usage Data**
- Pages visited
- Time spent on site
- Guides viewed

**Technical Data**

- Browser type and version

**Cookies and Tracking**

## Data Security

**Technical Measures**
- HTTPS encryption (all traffic)
- Password hashing (bcrypt)
- Secure session management
- Regular security updates
- Firewall protection

**Access Controls**
- Limited staff access
- Audit logs
- Two-factor authentication (coming soon)
- Regular access reviews

**Data Backup**
- Regular automated backups
- Encrypted storage
- Disaster recovery plan
- Geographic redundancy

<Warning>
  **No system is 100% secure**: While we implement industry-standard security, no internet transmission is completely secure. Use strong, unique passwords.
</Warning>

## Data Retention

### How Long We Keep Data

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|------------------|
| **Account info** | Until deletion requested |
| **Published guides** | Until deletion requested |
| **Comments** | Until deletion requested |
| **Usage logs** | 90 days |
| **Support tickets** | 2 years |
| **Deleted content** | 30 days (backups) |

### Deletion Requests

When you delete your account:
1. Immediate: Account disabled, data hidden
2. 30 days: Data marked for deletion
3. 90 days: Permanent deletion from backups

You can cancel deletion within 30 days.
